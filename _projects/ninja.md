---
layout: project
title: Ninja
description: >-
  Ninja is a smart phone companion that transforms your phone from a tool into
  a friend. Inspired by the ninja's spirit of endurance and perseverance, the
  device uses the Pomodoro technique to help users stay focused while building
  an emotional bond through anthropomorphic expressions, eye contact via face
  tracking, and contactless gesture interaction. The phone becomes Ninja's face,
  displayed on a 3D-printed body with servo-driven movements.
description_zh: >-
  Ninja 是一个智能手机陪伴产品，将手机从工具转变为朋友。灵感来源于忍者的坚韧精神，
  设备采用番茄钟工作法帮助用户保持专注，同时通过拟人化表情、基于面部追踪的眼神接触
  和非接触式手势交互建立情感联结。手机作为 Ninja 的面孔，嵌入 3D 打印的机身中，
  配合舵机驱动实现动态表达。
